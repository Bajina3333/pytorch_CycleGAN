In each row of the table, the three images on the left represent CBCT converted to CT and then reconstructed back to CBCT (CBCT → sCT → rec_CBCT), while the three images on the right represent CT converted to CBCT and then reconstructed back to CT (CT → sCBCT → rec_CT).

> Table Comparison:

The top row of the table for each patient's image slice shows those without paired L1 Loss Function, while the bottom row shows those with paired L1 Loss Function.

> Image analysis:

The sCT and sCBCT reconstructed images should resemble the original CT and CBCT images as closely as possible; similarly, the rec_CBCT and rec_CT reconstructed from sCT and sCBCT should also resemble the original CBCT and CT images as closely as possible.
